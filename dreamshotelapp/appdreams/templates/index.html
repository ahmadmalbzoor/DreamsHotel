Pullman ZamZam Makkah stands as a distinctive landmark in Makkah, located in the spectacular new Abraj Al Bait complex, directly adjoining Masjid Al Haram and the holy Kaaba in the very heart of Makkah facing King Abdulaziz gate. This masterpiece offers the finest hospitality in Makkah, having 1315 elegantly appointed rooms and suites with extravagant amenities, two contemporary dining options and five-star-deluxe services filled with spirituality while enjoying direct views over the holy Kaaba.

Makkah the holiest city in Islam, approximately 73 Kilometers inland from Jeddah-Saudi Arabia port of entry to the red sea. Makkah is one of the most magical places in all the world. Birthplace of prophet Mohamed (Peace be upon him ), Makkah is an ancient Bedouin gathering place that today has become a diverse and vibrant city, with inhabitants from many Islamic cultures.

Whether here during Hajj or Umrah the Grand Mosque is the destination for Islamic pilgrims from over the world, similarly the Kaaba (House of god) is the key focus for all who travel to Makkah. Another unmissable sight is “Jabal Al Nour” where prophet Mohamed (Peace be upon Him) received his first revelation from the archangel Gabriel in the cave of Hira. Also there is an extensive shopping collection including 450 stores within Abraj Albeit complex.
